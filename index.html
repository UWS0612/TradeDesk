Why this exists

Most trade software is a rental bill wearing a dashboard.

Generic SaaS tools promise convenience, then bury operators in recurring fees, irrelevant features, and workflows designed for somebody else's business.

The SaaS rental trap

Built for broad markets. Shaped around recurring revenue. Usually dependent on an account, internet, and ongoing fees.

  • Monthly cost that never stops
  • Feature bloat you do not need
  • Updates that disrupt your workflow
  • Vendor dependence and cloud reliance

The TradeDesk model

A delivered working system based on trade-domain logic. Direct, lean, and built to reduce admin friction instead of increasing it.

  • One-off purchase for the base system
  • Offline-capable local operation
  • Trade-specific workflow structure
  • Outside-scope changes handled by quote, not hidden tiers
